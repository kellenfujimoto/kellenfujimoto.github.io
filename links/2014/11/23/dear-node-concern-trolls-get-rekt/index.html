↗ Dear Node Concern Trolls: Get Rekt

Sun, Nov 23, 2014 ∞

I know this is a blunt response. I don’t have time to engage one-on-one with the armies of concern trolls spreading false equivalences and wasting time and emotional energy of the rest of the community. You may know them as the “Christian, dad, husband, programmer”s of the Internet. The people who think they are being victimized because they once heard that someone lost their job after being an asshole on the Internet, and feeling scared of and persecuted by this, without realizing that the position they enjoy in this industry is hugely propped up by financial and technical institutions built upon legacies of sexism, racism, and inequity - because it was (and remains) profitable.

No comment necessary. The toxic culture in tech (and especially online tech communities) demands preemptive measures to protect the greater community.
