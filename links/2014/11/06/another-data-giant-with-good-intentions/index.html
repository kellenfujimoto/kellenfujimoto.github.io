↗ Another data giant with good intentions

Thu, Nov 6, 2014 ∞

The Economist:

Skybox, a recently acquired division of Google, is […] promising free imagery for categories of work it deems “for good”.

It’s interesting how niche industries dominated by a few giant companies seem to always start off with the most altruistic goals. That Google is behind the helm of this company isn’t surprising — they’re even repurposing the “don’t be evil” rhetoric.
