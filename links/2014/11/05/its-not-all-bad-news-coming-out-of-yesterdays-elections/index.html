↗ It's not all bad news coming out of yesterday's elections

Wed, Nov 5, 2014 ∞

While national-level politics turned out abysmally, there’s some good news for my local friends:

  • Initiative 591 (outlawing further gun regulations): NO 68%
  • Initiative 594 (closing gun show loopholes): YES 75%
  • Seattle Proposition 1 (expanding transportation funding): YES 59%
  • Seattle Proposition 1B (universal preschool): YES 67%

So, while the nation must face the demagogues wresting control of both houses of congress, our little corner of the country will have an improving transportation system (lord knows we need it), will care for children in need, and provide basic checks for gun sales.

Source: King County Elections (PDF)
