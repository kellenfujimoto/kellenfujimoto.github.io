Let'S Encrypt Announces Free, Automated SSL Certificates Starting Next Summer

Tue, Nov 18, 2014

Let’s Encrypt is hopefully ushering in a new era of a more secure internet. The necessary precaution of acquiring a certificate is often too big a hassle — or too expensive — for small shops to bother with, even though going without is a huge risk for their users. I’m certainly rooting for them to succeed.
