About Me

I’m a (practically) native Seattleite of a decidedly nerdy persuasion; the fact that I have a blog would be a good indication of that, I guess. When I’m not typing things into here, I’m probably out jumping on stuff, making pretty photos, and tinkering with various software projects.

I live in a cute little SLU apartment with my partner Noël and our cat Bella. We’re both homebodys at heart, but we do find time to explore the area (she’s from the east coast) and sample the excellent culinary options available to us.

And this site?

I love building websites. This site is built on Hugo, a static site generator written in Go. Most posts for the time being will be on my various adventures in code slinging, and I’ll try to update this regularly with links I find interesting. Subscribe to the RSS feed or follow me on Twitter to see when I post, and thanks for reading!
